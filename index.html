About Us

A family bakery in the heart of Westport.

The Harbour Bakery started as a market stall in 2009. Fifteen years later we're still baking everything by hand, still using local flour, and still opening the doors at half seven every morning.

We believe good bread shouldn't be complicated. Just good ingredients, time, and a hot oven. Come in and see for yourself.
